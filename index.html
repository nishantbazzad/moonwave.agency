FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

How much work is delivered on every update?

The best way to answer this is that on every update you'll get about what an employee could realistically deliver in 2 days. Updates vary in size, we keep communications open at all times.

How many hours are included in subscription packages?

Our packages are focused on delivering results, not counting minutes. In fact, speed is our ally – the quicker we get it done, the more valuable it is for you. Expect updates or task completions every 2 days. We keep our client list short and sweet, giving the project love and attention it truly deserves.

Why prefer Moonwave over other agencies?

We're partners in your journey, not just service providers. We become embedded in your team, take ownership, and self-organize to deliver on projects that help achieve business goals. We're also a membership-based studio, meaning we have a limited number of seats. This allows us to focus on giving each client the best experience possible.

What if I don’t like my designs?

No problem at all! The great thing about the subscription is that we can revise your designs until you’re 100% happy with the result.

How can I manage tasks?

You’ll find a dedicated link in your Trello board to manage your subscription. You’ll login with the email used to sign up for the subscription. From there, you’ll be able to download invoices, and pause or cancel your subscription.

Can I cancel or pause my subscription?

Absolutely! You can cancel or pause your subscription at any time. Once you're ready to get started again, just email us or resume the subscription. .
